About Us

Mission

The Overcoming Cariño Scholarship is a lifeline for Latino students barred from federal grants due to their immigration status. By providing crucial financial support, it empowers them to break generational cycles of poverty and achieve their dreams. This transformative initiative also invests in parents, helping them pursue education and unlock opportunities, creating lasting change for entire families.

Vision

Break generational cycles of poverty by empowering Latino students and families through education, creating pathways to opportunity and lasting change.

Founder

Jonnathan Yepez Cariño is a visionary leader and community advocate dedicated to empowering others, particularly within the Latinx community. As the Founder of the Overcoming Adversity Cariño Scholarship, he champions educational opportunities for underserved students. He serves as CEO of Sin Fronteras Consulting LLC, offering strategic guidance to businesses and nonprofits. Jonnathan is VP Sr. Branch Manager for Pacific Premier Bank, driving financial inclusion and growth. Passionate about supporting his Latinx community, he works to ensure access to tools and resources for building generational wealth. He also leads as Co-President of the Greater Marysville Tulalip Chamber Foundation, and member of the Snohomish Community Foundation’s Finance Committee and board.

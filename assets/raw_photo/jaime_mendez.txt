<strong>Jaime Méndez</strong> is an Emmy award winner journalist, presenter, and influencer with a significant presence in the State of Washington. He is a pioneer and an icon in Spanish-language media in this region.

He was a News Anchor and reporter on the daily news program on Univision Seattle since its inception in 2007." His career in communication spans nearly 30 years, starting in local radio with news and community-focused programs.

Starting from January 1, 2024, Mendez started his journey as an independent journalist on social media under the company Se Habla Media, where Jaime Méndez serves as CEO.

Jaime Méndez, originally from Colombia, moved to the United States in 1987 and graduated in Communications and Social Sciences from The Evergreen State College.

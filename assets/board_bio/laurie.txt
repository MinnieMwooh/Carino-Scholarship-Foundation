<strong>Laurie Barbosa</strong>, the daughter of Mexican and Costa Rican immigrants, has served over 20 years as an Engineering Coordinator for the City of Marysville. A passionate advocate for social justice, she champions housing, education, mental health, and food security in Snohomish County.</br></br>
Graduating Magna Cum Laude with a 4.0 GPA from Western Washington University, Laurie is now pursuing a master’s in social work to expand her impact. She supports students through the Overcoming Adversity Cariño Scholarship Foundation and serves on multiple boards, including Millennia Ministries and the City of Marysville’s DEIAB Advisory Committee.</br></br>
Committed to equity and inclusion, Laurie works tirelessly to build a stronger, more just community.

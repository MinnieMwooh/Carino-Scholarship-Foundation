<strong>Jonnathan Yepez Cariño</strong> is a visionary leader and community advocate dedicated to empowering others, particularly within the Latinx community.
</br></br>
As the Founder of the Overcoming Adversity Cariño Scholarship, he champions educational opportunities for underserved students. He serves as CEO of Sin Fronteras Consulting LLC, offering strategic guidance to businesses and nonprofits. Jonnathan is VP Sr. Branch Manager for Pacific Premier Bank, driving financial inclusion and growth. Passionate about supporting his Latinx community, he works to ensure access to tools and resources for building generational wealth. He also leads as Co-President of the Greater Marysville Tulalip Chamber Foundation, and member of the Snohomish Community Foundation’s Finance Committee and board.

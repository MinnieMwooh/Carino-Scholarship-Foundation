<strong>Jaime Méndez</strong>, an Emmy-winning journalist and influencer, is a pioneer of Spanish-language media in Washington. With nearly 30 years in communications, he was the lead news anchor for Univision Seattle since its launch in 2007.
</br></br>
In 2024, he launched Se Habla Media, where he leads as CEO, bringing independent journalism to social media. A passionate advocate for youth and education, Jaime supports the Overcoming Adversity Cariño Scholarship Foundation to empower students.
</br></br>
Originally from Colombia, he moved to the U.S. in 1987 and earned his degree in Communications and Social Sciences from The Evergreen State College.
